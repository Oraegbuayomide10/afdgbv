Did You Know

Sexual Violence, Sexual Harrasement and Domestic Violence are majority of the GBVs which the Gender Based Violence Laws enacted in Namibia battle

NAMIBIA

  • DID YOU KNOW?

    The Combating of Domestic Violence Act (No. 4 of 2003) makes domestic violence a specific crime, and has a broad definition of domestic violence that includes physical abuse, sexual abuse, economic abuse, intimidation, harassment and serious emotional, verbal or psychological abuse.

  • DID YOU KNOW?

    The Combating of Domestic Violence Act (No. 4 of 2003) provides for the issuing of protection orders in domestic violence matters; creates the offence of domestic violence and provides for police duties in respect of domestic violence incidents.

  • DID YOU KNOW?

    The Combating of Rape Act (No. 8 of 2000) is a progressive law on rape. The Act gives greater protection to young girls and boys against rape and also provides for stiffer minimum sentences for rapists.

  • DID YOU KNOW?

    Marital rape is an offence in the eyes of the law.
