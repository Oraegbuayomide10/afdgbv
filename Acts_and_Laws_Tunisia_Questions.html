Did You Know ?

Domestic Violence, Sexual Violence and Sexual Harassment are majority of the GBVs which the Gender Based Violence Laws enacted in Tunisia battle

  • DID YOU KNOW?

    According to the Ministry of Justice, each year since 2006, 6000 complaints of domestic violence have been registered. Of these 6000 complaints less than 20% are actually received in court, the others being withdrawn by the victim.

  • DID YOU KNOW?

    The law of Tunisia states that an offenses of morality and sexual harassment is punishable by six months imprisonment and a fine of one thousand dinars. Whoever publicly violates good morals or public morals by gesture or speech or intentionally interferes with others in a way that Undermines indecency.
