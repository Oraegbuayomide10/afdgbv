Did You Know

Child and Early Marriage, Sexual harassment, Sexual violence, Trafficking are majority of the GBVs which the Gender Based Violence Laws enacted in Benin battle

BENIN

  • DID YOU KNOW?

    Interministerial Order establishes penalties for perpetrators of sexual abuse in public or private general, technical or vocational secondary schools or educational establishments.

  • DID YOU KNOW?

    Act No. 2006-04 of 10 April 2006 sets out conditions for the displacement of minors and the suppression of child trafficking in the Republic of Benin.

  • DID YOU KNOW?

    Provision is made for criminal penalties against the perpetrators and accomplices of sexual harassment, and persons who fail to notify the authorities of incidents of sexual harassment known to them.

  • DID YOU KNOW?

    Act No. 2006-19 speaks on the suppression of sexual harassment and protection of victims in the Republic of Benin. It specifies that Sexual harassment exists where anyone repeatedly gives orders or conveys a message, or makes threats, imposes constraints, exerts pressure or uses any other means in order to obtain sexual favours for themselves or a third party from a person in a vulnerable or subordinate position, and against the will of the harassed person.

  • DID YOU KNOW?

    Marriage can only be contracted between a man aged at least eighteen (18) years old and a woman aged 18 and over, at least eighteen (18) years, except exemption from age granted for serious reasons by order of the president of the court of first instance at the request of the public prosecutor.
