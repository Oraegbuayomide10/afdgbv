SOUTH AFRICA

  • DID YOU KNOW?

    The Protection from Harassment Act (2011) was passed in December 2011. It provides that sexual harassment means unwelcome sexual attention from a person who knows or ought reasonably to know that such attention is unwelcome; implied or expressed promise of reward for complying with a sexually oriented request.
