Did You Know

Sexual and Domestic violence, Sexual harassment and Child Marriage, are majority of the GBVs which the Gender Based Violence Laws enacted in Algeria battle

ALGERIA

  • DID YOU KNOW?

    Law 084-11 of June 9, 1984, Article-13 forbids the wali, whether he is a father or other, to force the marriage of the person placed under his guardianship, just as he cannot marry her without his consent

  • DID YOU KNOW?

    The Charter on Peace and National Reconciliation specifically does not allow persons who committed rape to obtain amnesty under the Charter.

  • DID YOU KNOW?

    A person convicted of a sexual harrasment offence is subject to imprisonment of two months to one year and a fine of 50,000 to 100,000 dinars.
