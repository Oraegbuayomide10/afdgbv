The Ugandan Government has developed a National strategy to End Child Marriage (2014/2015 -2019/2020). The key strategic focus areas of the strategy are; (A) Improved policy and legal environment to protect children and promotion of the girl childâ€™s rights;
(B) Improved access to quality sexual and reproductive health services, education, child protection services and other opportunities;
(C) Changing dominant thinking and social norms related to child marriage in the communities;
(D) Empowerment of both girls and boys with correct information to enable them recognize child marriage and early pregnancy as a gross violation of their rights and take mitigating action;
(E) Coordination, monitoring and evaluation mechanisms for the implementation effective of the strategy.
