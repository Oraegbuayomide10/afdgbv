Did You Know

Domestic Violence, Child Marriage, and other harmful practices are majority of the GBVs which the Gender Based Violence Laws enacted in Malawi battle

MALAWI

  • DID YOU KNOW?

    The Marriage, Divorce and Family Relations law raises the age of marriage to 18 years old

  • DID YOU KNOW?

    The Marriage, Divorce and Family Relations law protects women, young, old and the girl child, against any form of abuse or violence-emotional, physical or otherwise-that are related to marriage, sexual relations and family.

  • DID YOU KNOW?

    The Marriage, Divorce and Family Relations law holds those parents, who marry their children off below the age of 18, accountable to the law and ensures their prosecution.
