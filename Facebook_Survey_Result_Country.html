Attitudes and Beliefs about Gender Equality

Statistics

(A) 80 percent of respondents in Sub-Saharan Africa agreed that men and women should have equal opportunities.
(B) Both men and women in Sub-Saharan Africa reported that an average of around 6 (out of 10) of their neighbors would also agree that “household expenses are the responsibility of the man, even if his wife can help him.
(C) Regarding men’s household roles, 45 percent of respondents in the region agreed with the statement, “Household expenses are the responsibility of the man, even if his wife can help him.
(D) 66 percent of respondents in Sub-Saharan Africa said they agree with the statement, “A woman's most important role is to take care of her home and children.” Similar proportions of men and women expressed agreement.
