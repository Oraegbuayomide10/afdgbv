Early & Child Marriage, FGM and Sexual violence are majority of the GBVs which the Gender Based Violence Laws enacted in Somalia battle

SOMALIA

  • DID YOU KNOW?

    Article 401 (3) of the Penal Code, Legislative Decree No. 5 states that whoever abducts or detains a person who is incapable of giving consent for purposes of carnal violence, lust, or marriage shall be subject to punishments such as one to three years in prison.

  • DID YOU KNOW?

    Article 398 (1) of the Penal Code, Legislative Decree No. 5 states that whoever uses violence or threats to have carnal intercourse with a person of the other sex shall be punished with imprisonment for five to fifteen years.

  • DID YOU KNOW?

    Article 398 (3) of the Penal Code, Legislative Decree No. 5 states that a punishment of five to fifteen years imprisonment is applied to public officers who, by abuse of power, have carnal intercourse with people who are under arrest or detained in custody under the said officer.

  • DID YOU KNOW?

    Article 401 (2) of the Penal Code, Legislative Decree No. 5 states that whoever, with violence, threat, or deceit, abducts or detains an unmarried person for the purpose of marriage, shall be punished with imprisonment from one to three years.
